# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Eva de Vil fans visiting a focused game hub from desktop or mobile. They want a quick, stylish way to discover light web games while staying inside Eva's world and having an obvious route back to the main Eva de Vil site.

## Product Purpose

Eva Games is a seductive, simple grid of games hosted at https://evagames.org/. It exists as a fan-facing brand extension: a polished launcher now, with room for 2048 and future games as they are added. Success looks like immediate orientation, clear game availability, and a memorable visual connection to Eva de Vil without generic arcade tropes.

## Brand Personality

Seductive, controlled, intimate. The tone should feel like a velvet-lined invitation: confident, adult, refined, and a little dangerous without becoming explicit or gaudy.

## Anti-references

Do not look like generic gaming neon, casino bonus pages, childish arcade portals, crypto-gaming dashboards, or templated SaaS card grids. Avoid loud rainbow gradients, neon cyberpunk clichés, cheap badges, and cluttered ad-network aesthetics.

## Design Principles

1. Desire before density: show only enough to make each game feel wanted.
2. Control the room: use restraint, hierarchy, and negative space instead of loud gaming tropes.
3. Brand first, launcher second: the grid is simple, but the atmosphere belongs to Eva de Vil.
4. Make placeholders intentional: future games should feel reserved, not broken or forgotten.
5. Keep the way back obvious: the main evadevil.com link should be prominent and elegant.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-visible focus states, responsive layouts, descriptive labels, and reduced-motion support. Keep the seductive mood in color, spacing, and copy rather than relying on flashing, vibration, or low-contrast text.
